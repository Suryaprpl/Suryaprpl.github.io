Lets get to know some of the
japanese kana

hiragana

あ

Hiragana is used to write native Japanese words, words that do not have kanji, and particles in sentences. So, almost all words that are not written with kanji will use hiragana

katakana

ア

Katakana is usually used to write words of foreign language origin (such as English words accepted into Japanese), scientific names, onomatopoeia (words that describe sounds), and place names outside of Japan.
